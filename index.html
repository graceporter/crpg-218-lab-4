About Bonut Inc.

The shop is renowned for its signature donuts, which are meticulously crafted and change seasonally to keep things exciting. One of the favorites is the "Triple Berry Bliss," a donut filled with a luscious blend of raspberries, blueberries, and strawberries, topped with a sweet berry glaze.

Our lemon curd donut is a delicious pastry featuring a golden-brown exterior with a slightly crispy texture. Its soft and fluffy interior is complemented by a burst of vibrant and zesty lemon curd filling, creating a perfect balance of sweetness and acidity. Topped with powdered sugar or lemon-flavored icing, the donut offers a delightful aroma of fresh lemon and is often enjoyed with coffee or tea for a harmonious flavor experience.

Our Boston cream donut is a delectable pastry with a golden-brown exterior and a soft, pillowy texture. Its signature feature is a creamy vanilla custard filling that adds a luscious and sweet contrast to the dough. The donut is typically topped with a smooth chocolate glaze, creating a perfect harmony of flavors and textures. This classic treat offers a delightful combination of rich chocolate, velvety custard, and fluffy donut goodness.

Our cinnamon swirl donut is a delectable pastry characterized by its golden-brown exterior and soft, fluffy interior. The highlight is a generous swirl of cinnamon sugar, creating a sweet and aromatic filling throughout the donut. Topped with a light glaze or powdered sugar, the donut offers a perfect balance of warm cinnamon flavor and sweet dough. This delightful treat provides a comforting and indulgent experience for those who appreciate the classic combination of cinnamon and sugar.
